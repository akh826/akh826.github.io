PC & Browser Info

Collects information your browser is allowed to share about this device and session. Useful for debugging, support tickets, or checking your environment before an interview or deploy.

For security, websites cannot read everything on your PC (exact CPU model, RAM, serial number, etc.). This tool shows browser APIs only—screen size, timezone, user agent, CPU core count, WebGL GPU hint, and more where supported.
